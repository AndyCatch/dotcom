  • Verizon Business / Consumer Group (VBG+VCG) was a fast-moving strategy-led project that audited and then visualized key moments a Verizon retail and small business customer comes into contact with. Extensive UX and strategy sessions mapped these moments out, and agency to break out of the rigidity of the Verizon Design System was encouraged. This included embracing the image as a background texture, celebratory splashes of red (previously used less auspsiciously), as well as a few new takes on UI elements and UX patterns.
